Who I Am

I'm Naveen, a UI/UX Designer based in Jaipur, India

I've spent the last four years designing products across ed-tech, healthcare, HR tech, and restaurant tech — which means I've learned to move fast without losing rigor. I care most about the parts of design that are invisible when done well: the information hierarchy, the load time, the one extra tap someone didn't have to make.

Outside of client work, I maintain a personal design system, contribute UI kits to the community, and I'm currently sharpening my front-end skills so I can take a design further into production myself.
