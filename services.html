How It Works

My Process

01 — Discover
Understand the problem

Stakeholder interviews, competitor teardown, and a look at whatever data already exists.

02 — Define
Frame what to build

Turn research into a clear brief: what we're solving, for whom, and how we'll know it worked.

03 — Design
Wireframe to high-fidelity

Structure first, then visual system — moving from low-fidelity flows to polished, dev-ready screens.

04 — Deliver
Handoff & support

Specs, component libraries, and QA support through to launch — not a file dump at the end.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a typical project take? +

Most engagements run 4–10 weeks depending on scope — a landing page redesign sits at the shorter end, a full product design system at the longer end.

Do you also build the front-end? +

Yes, for HTML/CSS/JS and React builds. For larger engineering efforts I design to spec and work closely with your dev team through QA.

What do you need from me to get started? +

A short brief on the problem you're solving, access to any existing analytics or user feedback, and one point of contact for decisions.

Can you work with an existing design system? +

Absolutely — I'll audit what exists first and extend it rather than starting over, unless a rebuild is genuinely the right call.
